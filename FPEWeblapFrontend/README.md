# frontend

This website is being created for the "Fiatalok Pécsért Egyesület" (Youth for Pécs Association), which is a dynamic, user-friendly platform where visitors can get information about the organization's activities, upcoming programs, and news. The homepage starts with an attractive banner highlighting the organization's motto and mission. The program calendar is easily accessible, providing direct links to Facebook events, while the news section includes links to relevant Facebook posts. The 'Gallery' page showcases images of the organization's activities. The 'Contact' menu offers the organization's contact information and the possibility of sending messages directly through the website. The design of the site is modern and user-centric, focusing on engaging and informing young people.
